For Individuals:

Volun-tinder makes it easy for you to connect with local organizations and non-profits, so you can better engage with your community and your neighbors.

For Organizations:

Volun-tinder facilitates the volunteer registration process and provides your organization with a steady stream of like-minded individuals who are able to support your operations.
